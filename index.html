Map your
network strength

Biobridge is building an interactive map of our collective network across the life science industry. Your task is to rate the strength of your personal relationship with each contact shown.

The profiles you will see are drawn from the LinkedIn connections you submitted. They have been pre-filtered for life science relevance, so you are only scoring a focused subset — not your entire network.

Rate each
relationship

Score each contact based on the strength of your personal relationship using the four levels below.

👋

1 · Loose

You are connected but have had little meaningful interaction. They may not remember you well.

🤝

2 · Professional

You have worked together or exchanged meaningfully. They would respond to a message from you.

⭐

3 · Strong

You would pick up the phone for each other. Trust exists, and you could ask for a warm introduction.

🗑️

Remove

Not relevant or misclassified. Removes the contact from your scoring queue.
